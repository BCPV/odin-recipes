Menemen

Description

Menemen is a Turkish dish that can easily cooked and mostly consumed at breakfasts.

Ingredients

  • Two spoons of cooking oil
  • Three green peppers
  • Three tomatoes
  • Half teaspoon salt
  • Three eggs
  • Cheese (Amount is optional)

Steps

  1. Add oil and peppers to the pan and fry until the peppers change color.
  2. Add peeled and finely chopped tomatoes.
  3. Cover the pan on low heat and wait for the tomatoes to cook thoroughly.
  4. After the tomatoes are cooked, add the eggs.
  5. Add salt and spices of your choice and wait until the eggs are cooked.
  6. If you want to add cheese, you can add it at this stage.
